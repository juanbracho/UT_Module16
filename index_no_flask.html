Stock Analysis of the top companies for Oil and Telecom Sector in 2021

Oil Sector Data

This chart shows various financial metrics for three major oil companies: Exxon, Chevron, and ConocoPhillips. You can use the dropdown to explore different data points such as Adjusted Close Price, Stock Volume, Opening Price, Highest Price, Lowest Price, and more. Understanding these metrics helps analyze overall trends and performance in the oil sector over the past year.

Oil Results Data

This chart presents calculated metrics such as stock price percentage changes, quarterly volumes, return on investment (ROI), and stock volatility for major oil companies. These metrics help to evaluate the performance of each company and make informed investment decisions.
